It's not easy to keep on top of every new technological breakthrough, industry trend and hype cycle.

AI, ChatGPT, Midjourney, Web3, Web4, NFTs, Generative Art, Blockchain, LLMs, ML models - the list of technologies that are vital to success seems to go on and on and to grow longer with every passing week.

If you are an agency working to give your clients the best service, if you're a new brand looking for opportunities in rapidly changing markets or if you're an established business keen to keep up with the convergence of legacy media and new outlets - you need people on your side who know what's going on, what's coming next and what to do with it.

That's where Shorty comes in.

We are a women-owned creative and strategic consultancy at the intersection of art, culture and technology.

We are enlightened generalists fluent in the languages of new media, entertainment and convergence culture. Our guiding principle is to inspire the world around us by creating unconventional content and innovative programs that lead people to engage.

As strategists, connectors and producers, we have deep-rooted relationships with technologists, artists, celebrities, makers, brands and media platforms. We are often brought in to consult on integrated brand strategies, influencer and music partnerships, experiential activations and production.
