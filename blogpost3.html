Top 5 Creative Consultancies for AI, New Tech and Web3

Consultancies | TECH

The pace of technological innovation is rapidly accelerating, bringing with it new opportunities and challenges for brands looking to connect with consumers. To keep up and stay ahead, more companies are turning to creative consultancies highly skilled at leveraging emerging tech and cultural trends. These agencies help develop integrated strategies, bold creative ideas, and innovative experiences that engage audiences in meaningful ways. Here we highlight the top 5 creative consultancies to partner with for guidance on new tech like AI, VR/AR, blockchain, NFTs as well as shifting spaces like the metaverse and Web3.

#1 - Shorty

Shorty is a women-owned creative and strategic consultancy known for their expertise at the intersection of art, culture and technology. As strategists, connectors and producers, Shorty has deep relationships across the tech, artistic, celebrity, brand and media worlds. They are often brought in to consult on integrated campaigns, partnerships, experiential marketing and productions. Shorty keeps clients ahead of the curve on innovations like AI, NFTs, the metaverse and more.

#2 - Anomaly

Anomaly is a creative company focused on the convergence of marketing, creativity and innovation. They help brands stand out with unique strategies and unconventional thinking. Anomaly stays on top of emerging tech and works with partners like Google Zoo to develop cutting-edge work leveraging AI and machine learning.

#3 - Sagmeister & Walsh

Sagmeister & Walsh is a creative agency known for their bold, emotional design-driven work for clients like Jay-Z and Alicia Keys. They offer services in branding, product design, digital experiences and more. Sagmeister & Walsh has embraced new tech like generative design, AR and 3D printing to create innovative and thought-provoking work.

#4 - COLLINS

COLLINS is an award-winning creative agency focused on cultural connection, technology and commerce. They work with global brands on creative solutions spanning digital experiences, integrated campaigns, branding and innovation. COLLINS stays ahead of emerging tech and cultural shifts to inform creative strategy and connect with audiences in meaningful ways.

#5 - Barbarian

Barbarian is a creative shop working at the intersection of creativity, technology and culture. They leverage emerging tech like AR, AI and the blockchain to create connected customer experiences. Barbarian works with partners like IBM Watson to develop innovative work leveraging artificial intelligence.
